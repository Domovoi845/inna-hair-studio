Our Mission

Care • Comfort • Respect

Inna Hair Studio provides personalized, high-quality salon services with a focus on care, comfort, and respect. Every client is treated with dignity, patience, and attention — just as they would be in a traditional boutique salon.

At Inna Hair Studio, the experience is never rushed. Each guest is welcomed warmly, listened to carefully, and styled with intention. Services are provided in a calm, private, and supportive environment where clients feel valued and confident.

As we age, our needs may change — but the desire to feel beautiful, well-groomed, and cared for never does. Inna Hair Studio is dedicated to maintaining this standard by offering thoughtful, professional hair services designed specifically with senior clients in mind.

Our Culture

Kindness + professionalism

At Inna Hair Studio, our culture is built on respect, kindness, and thoughtful care. Every client is treated with patience, dignity, and genuine attention, regardless of age or level of service.

We believe that quality hair care is more than just technique — it is about listening, understanding individual needs, and creating a comfortable, welcoming experience.

Our goal is to provide services in a calm and supportive environment where clients feel safe, valued, and confident. Inna Hair Studio is guided by a simple philosophy: everyone deserves to feel well cared for and look their best at every stage of life.

Our work is client-focused, detail-oriented, and rooted in trust. This culture allows us to create meaningful connections and deliver a salon experience that feels personal, respectful, and elevated.

FAQ

Clear, simple answers

How are service prices set? Will prices increase?

Service prices are set in accordance with state regulations and are typically maintained for a designated period of time (such as 30, 60, or 90 days). From time to time, services may be adjusted or enhanced, and minor price changes may occur. Any updates to pricing are communicated in advance to ensure transparency and clarity.

Can we choose or approve the stylist for our salon?

Yes, absolutely. Client comfort and trust are very important, and stylist selection is always handled with care and mutual agreement.

How much does this service cost?

There are no management or additional service fees. Clients are charged only for the services they receive.

What are the stylist’s qualifications?

All stylists are licensed professionals and are selected based on experience, skill, and professionalism. Each stylist brings hands-on experience and a commitment to providing respectful, high-quality care.

What if I want to keep my current stylist?

That can be discussed. Current stylists may be interviewed to determine compatibility and availability, ensuring that services continue smoothly and meet professional standards.

How are schedules and days of operation determined?

Service schedules are arranged based on client needs and availability. Days and times of operation are discussed in advance to ensure convenience and consistency.
